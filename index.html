A Biography of Etue Felicitas

In Memory Of A lover

Mrs Felicitas was an inspiring soul, always there for all who came to her with their troubles. Her home was open to everyone, and her hospitality made them feel important. She was the most loving, humble, compassionate, understanding, family-orientated woman and one of the most beautiful souls one could ever meet. I am going to miss you so much, mum.

Mrs Etue was the Second Child of her parents, she was born on February 23Rd 1972. she attended 'Our Lady's schools and after her secondary school she moved on to study child care and Education in the Delta state University Abraka. she had 4 sisters and 3 brothers . Her parents Names are Mr & Mrs Clarke Dickson and Clarke Helen.


Mrs Felicitas married Mr Etue Felix and they Birthed five Wonderful children including the one writing this biography that you are reading. she worked as a primary school teacher for over 16 Years. she was a definition of a virtuous woman as stated in proverbs. She was Hardworking , caring, lovely, Kind, wise ,humble and many more attributes did she possesed.


Mrs Felicitas got sick mid-Year 2018, she undergoed Surgery and took many medications but all to No avail . she died at the early hours of November 21st 2018. she was and is still Greatly Loved bu All.
